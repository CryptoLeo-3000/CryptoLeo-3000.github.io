Work Description:

Insurance Agent Portal is an online insurance system which connects the customer to the agent and also view his services. Thus, minimizing the agentâ€™s work. The documentation for the same was made using Software Engineering methods and covers the project from requirement to testing phase. The project is part of academic project during Semester 3 for subject Software Engineering.

The website design has the following features:

  • Our Services -

    • Displays services of our website in brief.

  • EMI Calculator -

    • Customer can calculate their monthly EMI by entering the Insurance Amount, Time period and Interest Rate.

  • Contact Us -

    • COntact details for reaching out to the agent via Email, Phone along with his/her office address. An appointment with the agent can also be made by the appointment now button which opens to a google form to fill details.

  • Payments Page -

    • The page has payment details of the agent for netbanking/neft transaction. The transaction can also be verified by entering details liks transaction ID, name etc.

  • About Us -

    • Gives details about the agent, his vision and mission along with services that he provides.

  • Chatbot -

    • A mini simple guidance system located at bottom right of screen to provide navigation services to first time users.
