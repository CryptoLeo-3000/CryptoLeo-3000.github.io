About the Organization:

I.I.M.U.N. (India's International Movement to Unite Nations) is the worldâ€™s largest youth-run organisation that aims to bring the world closer, the Indian way. I.I.M.U.N. empowers the youth by enabling them to solve glocal problems at an individual level. The organisation comprises of 26,000 young organisers who put together student conferences in 160 cities across 22 countries, and work with over 7,500 schools impacting 10 million students. The organization simulates the workings of Indian Parliament and multi-lateral organisations for school students to engage them in debates about international relations, current affairs and world politics. The aim of the organisation is to get Indian students between 13 to 19 to become global citizens with an Indian mind-set.

Job Description:

In my time at I.I.M.U.N. we tasked to get delegates, guests for conferences and take part in various initiatives by the organisation. Along with work of delegation I was part of a project for designing an app for the organisation for Android and iOS. The app was designed using Figma designing tool.
The app design had the following features:

  • Home Page -

    • Where social media posts of the organization could be viewed every time a post went up on any one of its social media page.

  • About Page -

    • Public could view and learn about the organization and the work done by them.

  • I.I.M.U.N. TV -

    • A one place where various speeches, podcasts and interviews of famous personalities and Founder of the Organization, Mr. Rishabh Shah could be watched.

  • Conference, Events & Activities -

    • People could view past events of the organization and also book participation for upcoming events of teh organisation.

  • One world -

    • An exclusive magazine of the organisation which is published every month.

  • Contact Page -

    • Contacts for reaching out to the organisation with Email ID, address, Mobile numbers of the department heads and a google map snippet.
